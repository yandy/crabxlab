WHY

Internet of Things ( IoT ) is going to change the world.

We established CrabxLab™ to be part of this revolution and make it happen.

HOW

CrabxLab™ began with a simple idea: objects should be able to communicate and getting smart.

We aim to create an imaginative community of dreamers, thinkers, and doers. With cohesively teamwork and strong technology & design support to explore possibilities. We are committed to technology & design innovation in IoT. And we contribute more to inspire innovations in the IoT industry.

We are at the beginning of the revolution and we just get started.

We are a bunch of young people who have geek spirit, we explore unknown world and dig deeply into it to try out possibilities.

We are dreamers, thinkers as well as doers who believe in making the world a better place using the power of technology & design.
